FAQ'S
  • Are there internship opportunities available?

    Yes, we offer internship opportunities for select courses. These internships allow students to gain hands-on experience, build their portfolios, and apply what they've learned in a real-world setting.

  • Can I pay in installments?

    Yes, we offer flexible payment plans, allowing you to pay in installments. You can choose a plan that fits your budget and pay over time, ensuring that financial constraints do not hold you back from achieving your educational goals.

  • Do I need prior coding experience?

    No prior coding experience is required for all our courses. We offer courses that start from the basics, making them suitable for those new to coding.

  • Can I take multiple courses at the same time?

    Yes, you can enroll in multiple courses simultaneously. However, we recommend carefully considering your schedule and workload to ensure you can manage the course requirements effectively.

  • Is there a certificate upon completion?

    Yes, upon successful completion of a course, you will receive a certificate that you can use to showcase your new skills to potential employers or add to your professional portfolio.

  • What are the prerequisites?

    The only prerequisite is having a laptop. All other resources and tools will be provided or accessible online.

  • Do you offer no-code courses?

    Yes, we offer no-code courses in UI/UX Design and Data Analysis. These courses are designed to teach essential skills without requiring any coding knowledge.
