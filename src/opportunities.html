International Degree at Middlesex University

Enroll in our ADSE (Advanced Diploma in Software Engineering) program and get the chance to complete a one-year top-up degree at Middlesex University. This opportunity allows you to earn a BSc in just one year and gain international recognition.

Each year, we also hold a raffle draw where you could win up to a 50% scholarship for the ADSE program, making it easier for you to pursue your dream of an international degree.

Internship Opportunities

As part of our commitment to helping students gain real-world experience, we partner with leading companies to offer exclusive internship opportunities. Gain hands-on experience, work on real projects, and kickstart your career in the tech industry with our tailored internship placements.

These internships provide an invaluable opportunity to network, build your portfolio, and potentially secure a full-time position after your program.
